Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid-ul-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, a month of fasting. It is a day of celebration, prayers, and feasting with family and friends. Special dishes are prepared, and people often exchange gifts and charity.

Eid-ul-Azha

Eid-ul-Azha commemorates the willingness of Ibrahim (Abraham) to sacrifice his son as an act of obedience to God. This festival involves the sacrifice of animals, and the meat is distributed among family, friends, and those in need.

Eid Milad-un-Nabi

Eid Milad-un-Nabi celebrates the birth of Prophet Muhammad. It is observed with special prayers, gatherings, and processions. Devotees express their love for the Prophet through hymns and recitations.
